# Customer RFM Analysis (Python)

## Problem Statement
Analyze customer transaction data to segment customers and identify high-value users using RFM analysis.

## Steps Covered
- Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
- RFM Calculation (Recency, Frequency, Monetary)
- RFM Scoring & Customer Segmentation
- Visualizations
- Business Insights & Conclusion

## Tech Stack
- Python
- Pandas, NumPy
- Matplotlib, Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

## Outcome
- Identified Champions, Loyal, At Risk, and Lost customers
- Revenue contribution by segment
- Pareto analysis of top customers
